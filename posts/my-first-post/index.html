Learning about business

26.04.2020 11:52

If you’re just looking to learn more about different business activities and how they’re performed, there are many, many books to choose from, from high-level overviews to very focused. Scroll through the other comments if you want some recommendations.

If you’re actually interested in starting a business, understand that you don’t learn to play a sport from the sidelines. There are far more business spectators than participants, and they all have their favorite theories about what it takes to be successful, and they even write them down in books or on message threads, like this one. Even experienced entrepreneurs will admit that it’s hard to separate what they did from good luck.

So, if you want to start a software business, you need to sell your software, somehow.
